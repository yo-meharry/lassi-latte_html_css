Our Story

a lounge build on love & tradition

LLL was founded in 2024 with a simple belief “the best conversations happen over great drinks”. Built with a love for refreshing lassi & rich coffee ☕🥤, we created a space where the timeless cool of Punjab's yogurt traditions meets the warm ritual comfort of a proper espresso.

🥤 Where did Lassi originate?

Lassi originated in the Punjab region of the Indian subcontinent, with roots dating back to around 2,000 B.C. Traditionally crafted as a blend of yogurt, water, and spices - a drink born from necessity and perfected by love.
